Welcome to our site

Friends of Denton Dene meet as friends to co-ordinate with Urban Green and fellow park users about the continuous upkeep and improvement of our green and pleasant park. We meet regularly so we can all be informed and involved as to what is happening in the Dene and join with others in the park activities.
